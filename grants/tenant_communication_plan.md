# Tenant Communication Plan
## 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE Renovation Project

## Communication Goals
1. Provide clear, timely information about renovation plans
2. Address tenant concerns proactively
3. Minimize disruption to tenant daily lives
4. Build support for the renovation process
5. Educate tenants about energy efficiency benefits
6. Ensure compliance with grant program requirements

## Communication Timeline

### Phase 1: Initial Notification (Pre-Application)
**Timing**: 3-4 months before construction begins

**Communication Methods**:
- Individual tenant meetings
- Written notice (hand-delivered)
- Property-wide informational meeting

**Key Messages**:
- Overview of planned improvements
- Potential timeline (general)
- Benefits to tenants (lower utility bills, improved comfort)
- Income verification requirements for grant eligibility
- Assurance that rents will remain stable
- Contact information for questions

**Materials to Prepare**:
- Initial notification letter
- FAQ document
- Income verification forms
- Simple visual overview of improvements
- Consent forms for income verification

### Phase 2: Application Approval & Planning
**Timing**: 1-2 months before construction begins

**Communication Methods**:
- Follow-up individual meetings
- Written updates
- Property bulletin board
- Email/text updates (if preferred by tenants)

**Key Messages**:
- Funding approval status
- More specific timeline
- Detailed scope of improvements
- How improvements will affect each unit
- Temporary relocation needs (if any)
- Construction hours and expected disruptions

**Materials to Prepare**:
- Detailed improvement plan with visuals
- Construction schedule
- Contractor introduction and credentials
- Temporary accommodation details (if needed)
- Updated FAQ document

### Phase 3: Pre-Construction
**Timing**: 2-4 weeks before construction begins

**Communication Methods**:
- Pre-construction meeting (all tenants)
- Unit-specific written notices
- Text message alert system setup

**Key Messages**:
- Final construction schedule
- Unit access requirements and schedule
- Security measures during construction
- How to report concerns or issues
- Emergency contact information
- Specific disruptions (water, electricity, etc.)

**Materials to Prepare**:
- Detailed construction calendar
- Contractor contact cards
- Property manager emergency contact
- Unit preparation checklist for tenants
- Accommodation details for work hours

### Phase 4: During Construction
**Timing**: Throughout construction period

**Communication Methods**:
- Weekly written updates
- Text alerts for schedule changes
- Regular check-ins with each tenant
- 24-hour notice for unit access
- Suggestion/concern box in common area

**Key Messages**:
- Progress updates
- Schedule adjustments
- Upcoming work affecting specific units
- Addressing concerns that arise
- Celebrating milestones

**Materials to Prepare**:
- Weekly update template
- Photo documentation of progress
- Issue resolution tracking form
- Satisfaction check-in form

### Phase 5: Post-Improvement
**Timing**: Within 2 weeks of completion

**Communication Methods**:
- Post-improvement celebration
- Individual unit walk-throughs
- Written materials on new features
- Energy efficiency workshop

**Key Messages**:
- How to use and maintain new features
- Energy conservation tips
- Expected utility savings
- Ongoing maintenance plans
- Feedback request
- Grant program compliance requirements

**Materials to Prepare**:
- User guides for new equipment
- Energy conservation tips
- Maintenance request procedures
- Feedback survey
- Certificate of participation

## Special Considerations

### Language and Accessibility
- Provide communications in tenants' preferred languages
- Ensure written materials are clear and non-technical
- Offer alternative formats for visually impaired tenants
- Schedule meetings at times accessible to working tenants

### Privacy Concerns
- Handle income verification with strict confidentiality
- Provide private meeting options for sensitive discussions
- Secure all tenant data according to privacy laws
- Obtain written consent for sharing any tenant information

### Temporary Relocation (If Needed)
- Provide at least 30 days notice
- Clear written explanation of accommodations
- Assistance with moving essential items
- Daily updates during relocation period
- Compensation for inconvenience (if applicable)

### Tenant Rights Education
- Clear information about rights during renovation
- Written assurance about rent stability
- Documentation of pre-existing unit conditions
- Process for reporting concerns or violations

## Communication Templates

### Initial Notification Letter

```
[Date]

Dear [Tenant Name],

I'm pleased to inform you about an exciting opportunity to improve our building at 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE. I am applying for grants from DeKalb County and other programs to make significant improvements to our property that will benefit all residents.

The planned improvements include:
• Energy-efficient windows and doors
• Improved insulation and air sealing
• HVAC system upgrades
• Energy-efficient appliances
• [Other key improvements]

These improvements will make your home more comfortable, reduce utility bills, and improve overall quality of life.

To qualify for these grants, I need to verify that our property meets income requirements. This will require collecting some financial information from each household. All information will be kept strictly confidential and used only for grant eligibility.

I invite you to an informational meeting on [Date] at [Time] in [Location] to learn more. I'll explain the process, timeline, and answer any questions you may have.

Please know that:
• Your rent will not increase as a result of these improvements
• Disruption to your daily life will be minimized
• You will not be permanently displaced

If you have immediate questions, please contact me at [Phone] or [Email].

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
```

## Feedback Mechanisms

### Regular Check-ins
- Weekly door-to-door check-ins during construction
- Anonymous suggestion box in common area
- Dedicated phone line for concerns
- Monthly satisfaction surveys

### Issue Resolution Process
1. Document all concerns immediately
2. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
3. Investigate and develop solution within 48 hours
4. Communicate resolution plan to tenant
5. Follow up to ensure satisfaction
6. Document resolution for future reference

## Post-Project Engagement

### Energy Efficiency Education
- Host workshop on maximizing benefits of improvements
- Provide printed guides for new systems and appliances
- Share monthly energy-saving tips
- Recognize and reward energy conservation efforts

### Community Building
- Host celebration upon project completion
- Create shared responsibility for maintaining improvements
- Develop system for ongoing feedback
- Regular updates on energy/cost savings achieved

## Compliance Documentation
- Document all communications for grant reporting
- Maintain tenant acknowledgments of notifications
- Record attendance at informational meetings
- Photograph before, during, and after conditions
- Collect testimonials about improvement impacts
