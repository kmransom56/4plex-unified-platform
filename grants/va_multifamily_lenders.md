## VA Loan Requirements for Multifamily Properties

When applying for a VA loan for the multifamily property at 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE, be aware of these specific requirements:

### Occupancy Requirements
- You must certify intent to occupy one of the units as your primary residence
- Must move in within 60 days of closing
- Must maintain occupancy for at least 12 months

### Property Requirements
- Property must be 2-4 units (your property is 4 units)
- All units must meet VA Minimum Property Requirements (MPRs)
- Property must be primarily residential (not commercial)
- Must have separate utility meters for each unit

### Income Calculations
- Lenders can count expected rental income from other units
- Typically 75% of verified market rents (25% vacancy factor)
- Rental income documentation requirements vary by lender
- Some lenders require landlord experience or reserve requirements

### Appraisal Requirements
- VA-specific appraisal required
- Must verify both property value and rental income potential
- All units must be inspected
- Property must meet health and safety standards

## Tips for VA Multifamily Loan Application

1. **Prepare Rental Income Documentation**:
   - Obtain market rent analysis from appraiser
   - Gather existing leases (if property is occupied)
   - Research comparable rental rates in Brookhaven

2. **Highlight Property Condition**:
   - Document any recent improvements
   - Be prepared to address any potential MPR issues
   - Have preliminary renovation plans ready

3. **Maximize Debt-to-Income Ratio**:
   - Use projected rental income to offset mortgage payment
   - Pay down other debts if possible
   - Document all income sources carefully

4. **Coordinate Multiple Funding Sources**:
   - Inform lender of all assistance programs you're applying for
   - Ensure lender has experience coordinating these programs
   - Get clear timeline for all funding sources

5. **Prepare for Additional Scrutiny**:
   - Multifamily VA loans often receive additional underwriting review
   - Be ready to provide more documentation than typical single-family loans
   - Respond quickly to all lender requests

2. **Screening Questions**:

3. **Documentation Review**: Have each lender review your:
   - Credit report

4. **Loan Estimate Comparison**: Request Loan Estimates from each lender and compare:

5. **Final Selection**: Choose based on:

   - Experience with assistance programs
   - Communication quality and responsiveness
