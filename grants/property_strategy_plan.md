## Funding Strategy Overview
For this property, we recommend a layered approach that combines multiple funding sources to maximize available resources while addressing all renovation needs. The strategy prioritizes:

1. First addressing health and safety issues
2. Then implementing energy efficiency improvements
3. Finally adding clean energy technologies

## Recommended Funding Sequence

### Phase 1: Initial Assessment and Planning (Months 1-2)
1. **Georgia Power Energy Assessment**
   - Program: Georgia Power Multifamily Program
   - Purpose: Professional energy audit to identify priorities
   - Cost: $75 (potentially reimbursable)
   - Timeline: Schedule immediately

2. **DeKalb County CDBG Pre-Application**
   - Program: DeKalb County CDBG
   - Purpose: Begin eligibility determination process
   - Timeline: Submit by next quarterly deadline
   - Key Requirements: Tenant income verification, property documentation

### Phase 2: Building Envelope and Systems (Months 3-8)
1. **DeKalb County CDBG Application**
   - Funding Target: $40,000-$50,000 per unit
   - Focus Areas: 
     - Roof repairs/replacement
     - Window and door replacements
     - Insulation
     - HVAC upgrades
     - Electrical system improvements
   - Timeline: Full application after pre-approval
   - Key Requirements: Detailed scope of work, contractor estimates

2. **Georgia Weatherization Assistance Program**
   - Funding Target: $6,500-$8,000 per unit
   - Focus Areas:
     - Air sealing
     - Additional insulation
     - HVAC tune-up or supplemental improvements
   - Timeline: Apply after CDBG pre-approval
   - Key Requirements: Tenant income verification, energy audit

3. **Georgia Home Energy Rebates**
   - Funding Target: $2,000-$8,000 per unit
   - Focus Areas:
     - Energy-efficient appliances
     - Heat pump water heaters
     - Smart thermostats
   - Timeline: Apply through participating contractor
   - Key Requirements: Pre- and post-improvement energy assessment

### Phase 3: Clean Energy Implementation (Months 9-12)
1. **Residential Clean Energy Credit**
   - Funding Target: 30% of solar installation costs
   - Estimated Value: $15,000-$25,000 (based on system size)
   - Timeline: Claim on tax return after installation
   - Key Requirements: System must be owned, not leased

2. **Low-Income Communities Bonus Credit**
   - Funding Target: Additional 10-20% of solar costs
   - Estimated Value: $5,000-$15,000
   - Timeline: Apply during IRS application window
   - Key Requirements: Tenant income verification, benefit sharing plan

3. **GRID Alternatives Multifamily Program**
   - Funding Target: Full solar system installation
   - Estimated Value: $40,000-$60,000
   - Timeline: Apply after building envelope improvements
   - Key Requirements: Affordability commitment, suitable roof condition

### Phase 4: Financing Gap Coverage (As Needed)
1. **Georgia HOME Investment Partnership**
   - Funding Target: $50,000-$100,000 (if larger rehabilitation needed)
   - Focus Areas: Comprehensive rehabilitation beyond energy improvements
   - Timeline: Apply based on annual NOFA
   - Key Requirements: Long-term affordability commitment

2. **Good Capital Fund's Georgia Bright Program**
   - Funding Structure: Low-cost solar lease
   - Purpose: Alternative if direct ownership not feasible
   - Timeline: Apply if other solar funding not secured
   - Key Requirements: Roof in good condition, benefit sharing plan
