## Key Programs for Small Multifamily Properties (2-4 Units)

### 1. Community Development Block Grant (CDBG)
This program is particularly relevant for small multifamily properties in Brookhaven as it's administered directly through DeKalb County. CDBG funds can be used for rehabilitation of existing multifamily properties, especially those serving low-income residents.

### 2. Georgia Weatherization Assistance Program
While traditionally focused on single-family homes, this program can also apply to small multifamily buildings (2-4 units). It provides comprehensive energy efficiency improvements that can significantly reduce utility costs.

### 3. Georgia's Home Energy Rebates
These rebates are applicable to small multifamily properties and can provide substantial financial assistance (up to $8,000 per unit) for energy efficiency improvements.

### 4. Residential Clean Energy Credit
This federal tax credit covers 30% of the cost of installing solar panels, which can be a significant incentive for multifamily property owners looking to adopt clean energy solutions.

## Clean Energy-Specific Incentives

### 1. Low-Income Communities Bonus Credit
This provides an additional 10-20% tax credit on top of the standard investment tax credit for solar installations in low-income communities or on qualified low-income residential buildings.

### 2. Georgia Power Multifamily Energy Efficiency Program
Offers specific incentives for multifamily properties to improve energy efficiency, which can complement solar installations.

### 3. Good Capital Fund's Georgia Bright Program
Provides low-cost solar leases specifically for Georgia residents, with a focus on making solar accessible to low-income communities.

## Application Priority Recommendations

Based on the research, property owners in Brookhaven should consider applying in this order:

1. **DeKalb County CDBG Program** - Local administration means potentially faster processing and specific focus on local needs
2. **Georgia Weatherization Assistance Program** - Can provide immediate energy efficiency improvements
3. **Georgia's Home Energy Rebates** - Substantial funding available per unit
4. **Federal Tax Credits** - Can be claimed when filing taxes after improvements are made

Each program has specific application requirements. Contact information is provided in the table above for direct inquiries about current application cycles and specific eligibility requirements for your property.
