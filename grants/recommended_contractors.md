## Tips for Contractor Selection

1. **Verify Program Approval**: Confirm the contractor is approved for the specific grant program you're applying for
2. **Check References**: Ask for references from similar multifamily rehabilitation projects
3. **Verify Licenses and Insurance**: Ensure all required licenses and insurance are current
4. **Review Experience**: Look for contractors with specific experience in energy efficiency and grant-funded projects
5. **Get Multiple Bids**: Obtain at least three bids for comparison
6. **Evaluate Communication**: Choose contractors who communicate clearly and promptly
7. **Understand the Timeline**: Ensure the contractor can meet grant program deadlines
8. **Review Contracts Carefully**: Have contracts reviewed by a legal professional before signing
