# Georgia Dream Down Payment Assistance Program
## Sample Application for 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE

This document provides a completed sample application for the Georgia Dream Down Payment Assistance Program, specifically tailored for a veteran purchasing a multifamily property in Brookhaven using a VA loan.

## Program Overview

The Georgia Dream Homeownership Program, administered by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs (DCA), provides down payment assistance to eligible first-time homebuyers and qualified veterans. Veterans can receive up to $10,000 through the PEN (Protectors, Educators, and Nurses) option.

## Special Considerations for Veterans

As a veteran applicant for the Georgia Dream program, you qualify for:

1. **PEN Program Enhanced Assistance**: Up to $10,000 (instead of standard $7,500)
2. **First-Time Homebuyer Exemption**: Veterans need not be first-time homebuyers
3. **Income Limit Flexibility**: Some VA benefits may be excluded from income calculations

## Next Steps After Submission

1. **Application Review**: 5-10 business days
2. **Conditional Approval**: If approved, valid for 45 days
3. **Homebuyer Education**: Complete required course
4. **Property Appraisal**: Ensure property meets program standards
5. **Final Approval**: Upon completion of all requirements
6. **Closing Coordination**: Funds sent directly to closing attorney

## Notes on Multifamily Property Considerations

For your multifamily property purchase at 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE, please note:

1. **Owner Occupancy Requirement**: You must occupy one unit as your primary residence for at least one year
2. **Income Calculation**: Rental income from other units may be considered in your total income calculation
3. **Property Standards**: All units must meet program property standards
4. **Appraisal Requirements**: Appraisal must support both the purchase price and the rental income projections
