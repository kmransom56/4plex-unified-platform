# DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program
## Sample Application for 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE

This document provides a completed sample application for the DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program, which can provide up to $10,000 in down payment and closing cost assistance for the purchase of 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE.

## Program Overview

The DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program provides financial assistance to eligible homebuyers for down payment and closing costs. The program is administered by the DeKalb County Community Development Department and funded through the HOME Investment Partnerships Program.

## Special Considerations for Veterans

As a veteran applicant for the DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program, you benefit from:

1. **First-Time Homebuyer Exemption**: Veterans with honorable discharge are exempt from the first-time homebuyer requirement
2. **Income Limit Flexibility**: Some VA benefits may be excluded from income calculations
3. **Compatibility with VA Loans**: This assistance can be used with your VA loan

## Special Considerations for Multifamily Properties

For your multifamily property purchase at 2072 E Roxboro Rd NE, please note:

1. **Owner Occupancy Requirement**: You must occupy one unit as your primary residence for the entire affordability period
2. **Rental Units**: Other units must be rented to income-qualified tenants at affordable rates
3. **Income Calculation**: Rental income will be considered in your total income calculation
4. **Property Standards**: All units must meet program property standards
5. **Landlord Requirements**: You will need to comply with program requirements for the rental units

## Next Steps After Submission

1. **Application Review**: 10-15 business days
2. **Income Verification**: DeKalb County will verify all income sources
3. **Property Inspection**: County will inspect the property for compliance
4. **Underwriting**: Financial review of application
5. **Conditional Approval**: If approved, valid for 60 days
6. **Homebuyer Education**: Complete required course
7. **Final Approval**: Upon completion of all requirements
8. **Closing Coordination**: Funds sent directly to closing attorney

## Affordability Period and Loan Forgiveness

The DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program provides assistance in the form of a forgivable loan. The loan is forgiven over time as long as you maintain the property as your primary residence.

**Assistance Amount:** $10,000  
**Affordability Period:** 5 years  
**Forgiveness Rate:** 20% per year  
**Repayment Requirement:** If you sell, transfer ownership, or no longer occupy the property as your primary residence during the affordability period, you will be required to repay the unforgiven portion of the loan.

## Combining with Other Assistance Programs

This program can be combined with:
- Georgia Dream Down Payment Assistance
- VA Loan benefits
- Seller concessions
- Federal tax credits for energy improvements

**Total Potential Assistance:**
- DeKalb County Program: $10,000
- Georgia Dream (PEN): $10,000
- VA Loan: 100% financing
- Seller Concessions: Up to $23,000 (4% of purchase price)

## Income Limits for Program Eligibility

DeKalb County First-Time Homebuyer Program uses HUD income limits based on household size. The current income limits (80% of Area Median Income) are:

| Household Size | Maximum Annual Income |
|----------------|------------------------|
| 1 person | $54,000 |
| 2 persons | $61,700 |
| 3 persons | $69,400 |
| 4 persons | $77,100 |
| 5 persons | $83,300 |
| 6 persons | $89,450 |
| 7 persons | $95,650 |
| 8 persons | $101,800 |

*Note: These limits are subject to change. Verify current limits with the program office.*
