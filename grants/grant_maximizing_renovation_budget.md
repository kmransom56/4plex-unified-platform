## Cost-Saving Strategies While Maintaining Eligibility

1. **Bulk Purchasing**: Negotiate discounts for materials across all units
2. **Phased Contractor Approach**: Keep crews working continuously to reduce mobilization costs
3. **Material Standardization**: Use same fixtures/finishes in all units
4. **DIY Components**: Identify non-technical work that can be done without contractors
5. **Utility Programs**: Leverage free services from utility companies
6. **Salvage Value**: Credit for recycling old appliances and materials
7. **Volunteer Labor**: Partner with organizations like Habitat for Veterans
